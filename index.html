Introduction

Liben Toms is a seasoned and versatile live and studio drummer from Bangalore, India. Predominantly playing music in church, he is a unique artist who has been associated with numerous acts that span across diverse genres from CCM, Funk-fusion, progressive metal, R&B and many more.

Some of the acts he is associated with are- Bryden Parth, Sheldon Bangera, REX Media.
